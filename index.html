about me

My name is Fer and I am a graphic and web designer/developer from Sydney. I was born and raised in Mexico City, and moved to Australia in 2012.

I started studying design and working as a print designer in Mexico, and studied a Diploma of Graphic Design at Design Centre Enmore TAFE.

I have worked in print and digital design in several industries. Although I had designed and built some websites in the past, the whirlwind that is 2020 gave me the time and space to really rediscover my love for creation and for web design and development in particular, pushing me to learn new skills and work for some new and exciting clients in new industries.

I am currently studying a Bootcamp of Full Stack Development and I am keen to work on new and exciting projects of all sizes.
